LAVISHGREEN

LAVISHGREEN is an online grocery delivery system that features a user-friendly website app. Users can register, browse products, add items to their carts, and place orders for quick delivery. The system also includes an admin interface for managing product categories, adding new items, and overseeing user accounts and orders. The aim is to provide a convenient and efficient solution for perishable goods shopping, ensuring a seamless online experience with prompt delivery.

TO DO LIST

TO DO LIST is a user-friendly task management project, offering an application for creating, prioritizing, and managing daily tasks efficiently. Users can easily add, edit, or delete tasks, set deadlines, and mark items as completed. The project aims to enhance productivity by providing a practical and accessible tool for individuals to stay organized and focused on their daily activities.

ASSET CARE HUB

Asset Care Hub is a comprehensive solution that simplifies ICT asset management challenges by improving tracking, predictive maintenance, and reporting. Developed using front-end and back-end technologies and an Agile methodology, it fills gaps in existing knowledge. The goal is to empower organizations to optimize asset use, reduce downtime, and leverage Artificial Intelligence for predictive maintenance, enhancing overall efficiency and competitiveness.
